# Big Billion Day Prep: Can Your Support Handle 10x Call Volume?

## The Problem Nobody Talks About

Priya ordered a dress at midnight during a flash sale. She paid COD. The delivery guy came on Tuesday at 2 PM. She was at work. He came again Wednesday. She was at her mother's place.

On Thursday, the order went back. Priya forgot she even ordered it.

## Let Me Tell You a Story

Meet Ananya. She runs a D2C skincare brand from her apartment in Indore. Ships about 800 orders a month. 60% are COD.

Her RTO rate? 35%. That's 168 orders coming back every month. At ₹150 per return, that's ₹25,200 gone. Plus the product that can't be resold. Plus the customer who might not order again.

Ananya tried SMS confirmations. 8% response rate. Email? 3%.

Then she tried something radical: a phone call in Hindi, 30 minutes after order placement. 'Namaste Priya ji, aapne order kiya tha... confirm karein?'

Her RTO rate dropped to 18% in the first month. Not because of magic. Because someone actually called and asked.

## What This Actually Means for Your Business

**Let's talk numbers.**

If you ship 1,000 COD orders a month and 35% come back as RTO, that's 350 failed deliveries. At ₹150 per return, you're losing ₹52,500 monthly just on logistics. Add the cost of the product (often unsellable), the repacking, the restocking — you're looking at ₹1.5-2 lakh in monthly losses.

A confirmation call in the customer's language, within 30 minutes of order placement, can cut RTO by 30-50%. Not because of magic — because the customer remembers they ordered, confirms the address, and commits to being available.

**The cost of that call? Less than the cost of the return.** That's not technology spending. That's math.

## How COD Confirmation Actually Works

**The problem:** Customer orders at midnight. Delivery attempt on Tuesday afternoon. Customer is at work. Order returns. Everyone loses.

**The fix:** A phone call within 30 minutes of order placement. Here's what happens:

1. **Order placed.** System triggers AI call automatically.
2. **AI calls in customer's language.** 'Namaste Priya ji, aapne Myntra se red kurta order kiya hai — ₹1,299, COD. Aapne hi order kiya na?' (Verify buyer intent — catches fake orders.)
3. **Address confirmed.** 'Address: 42, Gandhi Road, Indore — correct?' (Catches wrong addresses before shipping.)
4. **Delivery time checked.** 'Delivery Thursday 2-6 PM. Aap available hain?' (Reduces failed delivery attempts.)
5. **WhatsApp follow-up.** Tracking link sent immediately. Reminder 24 hours before delivery.

**The result:** RTO drops by 30-50%. Not because of technology magic — because someone actually called and confirmed. In the customer's language. At the right time.

The entire process takes 90 seconds. Costs less than the return shipping. And your customer feels like someone actually cares about their order arriving.
