## How to Actually Evaluate Voice AI ROI

Forget the fancy calculations. Here's simple math that any business owner can follow:

**The cost side:**
- Speaksy charges ₹2.5 per minute
- Average call: 3-4 minutes = ₹7.5-10 per call
- 1,000 calls = ₹7,500-10,000

**The value side:**
- Each answered call = one customer who didn't hang up
- If 10% of those become customers worth ₹5,000 each = ₹5,00,000 revenue
- Cost to generate: ₹10,000
- ROI: 50x

**But let's be conservative:**
- Say only 2% convert
- Say average order value is ₹2,000
- 1,000 calls × 2% × ₹2,000 = ₹40,000 revenue
- Cost: ₹10,000
- ROI: 4x

**Even more conservative — just saved costs:**
- Human agent cost per call: ₹50-80 (salary + overhead)

- Savings: ₹40-70 per call
- 1,000 calls = ₹40,000-70,000 saved

**The real ROI though?** It's the customer at 9:47 PM who got an answer instead of voicemail. The lead who got called back in 30 seconds instead of 2 days. The patient whose appointment was confirmed at 7 AM.

Some things don't fit in a spreadsheet. But they show up in your revenue.

## Try This Simple Calculation for Your Business

Grab a calculator. Answer these 5 questions:

1. **How many calls do you get daily?** (Check your phone logs)
2. **How many do you answer?** (Be honest)
3. **How many are enquiries/sales leads?** (Estimate)
4. **What's your average order value?** (You know this)
5. **What's your conversion rate?** (Leads who become customers)

Now:
- Unanswered calls × % that are leads × your conversion rate × average order value = **Monthly revenue you're losing**

Example:
- 100 calls/day × 40 unanswered = 40 missed calls
- 50% are leads = 20 leads/day
- 10% convert = 2 customers/day
- ₹5,000 average order = ₹10,000/day lost
- **₹3 lakh/month in lost revenue**

An AI voice agent costing ₹15,000-25,000/month that answers those 40 calls?

That's not an expense. That's the best ROI you'll see this year.
